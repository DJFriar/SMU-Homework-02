About Me

Nice to meet you visitor, I'm Tommy. This site is my portfolio site, where you can see a few of the projects I have worked on. Currently I'm focused on learning full stack web development, but I have published some mobile applications; both on Android and iOS.

Outside of coding I have two main hobbies: motorcycling and photography. I am an avid motorcyclist with a particular interest in long distance riding. Last year I became an Iron Butt member (IBA #72302), and this year have ridden over 15,000 miles in various motorcycle rallies. I also enjoy photography with a focus in architecture and fashion shooting. I mainly shoot Fuji X-Series cameras.

You can find me on Instagram and GitHub.
